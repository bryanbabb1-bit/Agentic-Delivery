STATEMENT OF WORK (excerpt) — Zennify SBH, Financial Services Cloud

Scope: Client 360.

Advisors at the bank currently hunt across multiple tabs to assemble a picture of
a retail client. This engagement delivers a unified "Client 360" profile in
Financial Services Cloud: a single Person Account page that surfaces the client's
financial accounts, financial goals, and recent activity together.

In scope (Zennify build): the Client 360 Lightning profile, the supporting page
layout and components, and SIT against a sandbox.

Out of scope / customer-owned: any real-time core-banking integration for the
activity feed. The data model for retail clients is assumed to follow FSC
defaults.

Deliverables: up to 2 sandboxes, a Jira story export, and SIT results. The
package is delivered UAT-ready for customer-led testing.
